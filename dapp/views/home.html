CoinOffering Ltd.

Dapp for CoinOffering cryptoshares

To use smart contract you have to open this page in Mist (you can download and install Mist on github.com/ethereum/mist/releases ).

Put address of this page (https://coinoffering.github.io/dapp/) to Mist browser address bar and click 'Enter'. Then click on the 'connect' in the upper right corner, and authorize one more blockchain accounts to work with this smart contract.



In Mist you can switch between MainNet and TestNet - balances in smart contract are, of course, different, but code and interface are the same.
